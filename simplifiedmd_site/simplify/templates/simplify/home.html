About SimplifiedMD

SimplifiedMD is a website useful for people that have a hard time understanding highly dense medical information! By inserting your desired text to simplify, our website is able to simplify your input text and output a simplified version of your medical text input. Simply copy any piece of text from a medical website such as WebMD, MedlinePlus, Mayo Clinic, and others, and just paste the piece of text into the text box on the main page and click on the "Simplify it!" button. A simplified version of text will then be output after the button has been pressed.

We believe that understanding medical information such as diseases and drugs is crucial for patients as they can have a sense of relief when it comes to minor diseases or a sense of awareness when it comes to complicated and high impact diseases/drugs. Therefore, having a good grasp of what is going on with your body is important and thus SimplifiedMD is perfect for novice users that would like to learn more about certain diseases and/or drugs!
